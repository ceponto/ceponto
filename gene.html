GENE

EN

Gene is the first number of a hypothetical magazine with a focus on a variety of religious organizations, tackling theories and revealing their true motivations. Each number compares two of these institutions, beginning the first number with Scientology and Flat Earth Society.

PT

Gene é o primeiro número de uma revista hipotética com foco sobre várias organizações religiosas, abordando teorias e revelando as suas verdadeiras motivações. Cada número coloca duas destas organizações numa espécie de confronto, inaugurando este primeiro número com a Scientology e a Flat Earth Society.

EN

Made up of spreads splited into two parts, this publication has two distinct indexes, related to both halfs of each spread (right page - Scientology and left page - Flat Earth Society). The subjects of each entity are related to one another in spite of being different in content.

PT

Constituída por spreads dividídos em 2, esta publicação possuí dois índices distintos, relativos a ambas as metades de cada spread (página direita - Scientology e página esquerda - Flat Earth Society). Os assuntos de cada uma relacionam-se apesar de serem referentes às entidades respetivas.
